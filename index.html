Solar Powered Boat

As part of an independent study at Davidson College, I co-led a student initiative to design and prototype a solar-powered pontoon boat aimed at demonstrating clean, efficient marine transportation. The project involved conducting technical and environmental feasibility analyses, optimizing solar array and powertrain configurations, and coordinating fabrication planning and materials sourcing. I worked with sponsors and faculty mentors to secure resources, refine the design, and move the project from concept toward a functional solution.
